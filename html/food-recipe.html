Scientific Benefits of Ridge Gourd

Ridge gourd, also known as "turai" or "sorakaya," is packed with vital nutrients and is highly regarded for its numerous health benefits. It is low in calories and rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Ridge gourd is excellent for weight management, improves digestion, and promotes skin health. Its high water content helps in keeping the body hydrated, and its anti-inflammatory properties contribute to boosting immunity.
